18.1 What Vimshottari Is — Definition · Source · Why This System१८.१ विंशोत्तरी म्हणजे काय — व्याख्या · स्रोत · हीच प्रणाली का

Condition / state line. "Vimshottari dasha" is the most widely used and most accurate planetary-period system in Vedic astrology — a fixed 120-year sequence of nine planetary "seasons" calibrated to the native's birth-Moon-nakshatra, attributed in its canonical form to Maharshi Parashara in the Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra, and used by virtually every Jyotish school for event-timing.

Possibilities under this condition. Jyotish recognises multiple dasha systems, each with its own logic and use-case. The major families: (1) Vimshottari — the standard 120-year nakshatra-based dasha, the system this chapter teaches and the only one used in chapters 19–27 of this book. (2) Yogini-dasha — an 8-period 36-year cycle based on lunar mansions; popular in north-Indian tradition for short-term-timing of fortune and misfortune. (3) Ashtottari-dasha — a 108-year, 8-graha (no Ketu) cycle used when the native is born in the bright-half (Shukla) of a day-and-night-and-pakshas matrix; secondary-confirmation dasha in classical readings. (4) Chara-dasha (Jaimini) — a rashi-based dasha (signs rule, not planets) used in the Jaimini tradition for life-stage prediction; foundational to the Karakamsha/Atmakaraka analysis. (5) Kalachakra-dasha — a complex nakshatra-pada based dasha attributed to Achyutananda; used by some south-Indian astrologers for inheritance and karmic-pattern readings. (6) Narayan-dasha, Mandook-dasha, Sthira-dasha, Shoola-dasha and dozens of others — each with niche applications. Among all these, Vimshottari is preferred for one reason: predictive accuracy when tested against observed life-events. Across the classical literature (Parashara, Mantreshwara, Varahamihira, Kalyana Verma) and across the practical record of working astrologers for the last fifteen-plus centuries, Vimshottari has been the system that lines up most precisely with what actually happens in a life — births, marriages, deaths, career-turns, health-events, financial-events. The historical source is the Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra, chapter 46-onwards, where Parashara names the system, gives the year-counts, gives the nakshatra-lord mapping, and gives the calculation method. Why nakshatra-based? Because the Moon is the karaka of mind/manas and the nearest celestial body to Earth — and the nakshatras are the finest-grained zodiacal division Vedic astronomy uses (27 divisions of 13°20′ each, versus the 12 rashis of 30°). The Moon's exact position at birth therefore captures the most-fine-grained karmic-timestamp; Vimshottari is the system that uses that timestamp directly.

(2) The Moon-position at 2° Aquarius in Dhanishtha nakshatra gives a clean, well-into-the-nakshatra timestamp — neither edge-case nor sandhi-zone — which means Vimshottari's balance-calculation is reliable and software-agreement is high. (3) The native's already-lived life-events (school-completion 2014, first job 2017–2018, career-promotion 2022, current Ju-Ve marriage-and-consolidation window 2025–2027) have lined up precisely with Vimshottari's MD/AD boundaries — confirmation by hindsight that this is the right system for this chart. Vimshottari is the Parashara-rooted 120-year nakshatra-based dasha that virtually every Jyotish school uses for event-timing; for this chart it is the natural fit because of the Krishna-paksha birth, the clean mid-nakshatra Moon-position, and the strong hindsight-alignment with already-lived events — chapters 19 to 27 of this book all run on this single timing-engine.

18.2 The 120-Year Cycle — Nine Lords · Year-Counts · Fixed Sequence१८.२ १२० वर्षांचे चक्र — नऊ स्वामी · वर्ष-गणना · निश्चित क्रम

Condition / state line. The Vimshottari cycle assigns 120 total years to the nine grahas — Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu — in a fixed sequence and with fixed year-counts that never change from chart to chart; what changes is which planet's period a given native enters at birth and how much of that period is already used up.

Possibilities under this condition. The nine lords, their year-counts, and their fixed sequence are: Ketu 7, Venus 20, Sun 6, Moon 10, Mars 7, Rahu 18, Jupiter 16, Saturn 19, Mercury 17 — total 120 years. The sequence loops: after Mercury comes Ketu again. Why these specific year-counts? Several classical explanations are offered. (a) Lifespan-proportionality theory: 120 years is the maximum human lifespan in Vedic anthropology (Manu's lifespan-classes go up to 120 years for the perfect age), and the nine lords distribute that span proportionally to their nature — Sun and Mars (the burning, brief, intense planets) get short periods (6 and 7); Venus and Saturn (the long-duration, embedded-in-life planets) get long periods (20 and 19); Rahu (the magnifier of desire, longest-shadow karmic-thread-holder) gets 18; Jupiter (the preserver, the most-protective benefic) gets 16; Mercury (the connector, the planet of intellect that adapts to context) gets 17; Moon (the mind that needs decade-rhythms) gets 10; Ketu (the sharp-burning ascetic-igniter) gets a brief intense 7. (b) Nakshatra-degrees theory: each planet rules three nakshatras, totalling 40° (since 3 × 13°20′ = 40°), and the year-counts approximately track each planet's effective influence-degree in a 360° zodiac after weighting for benefic/malefic nature. (c) Numerical theory: 120 = 5! (factorial of 5) which has deep significance in tantra-and-yantra mathematics; the distribution among nine planets follows divisibility-and-symmetry patterns that align with the Sri-Chakra and other yantric structures. Regardless of which theory one prefers, the year-counts and the sequence are fixed in classical practice and never adjusted. The sequence-rule has a practical consequence: from any given starting-planet, the next-planet is fully determined, which means once the birth-MD is identified, the entire 120-year forward-and-backward timeline can be written out mechanically.

18.3 Birth-Mahadasha Calculation — Moon's Nakshatra · Balance Formula१८.३ जन्म-महादशा गणना — चंद्राचे नक्षत्र · शिल्लक-सूत्र

Condition / state line. The birth-mahadasha is determined entirely by the nakshatra in which the natal Moon sits, and the residual-balance of that birth-MD (the years still remaining when the native is born) is computed from how much of the nakshatra-arc the Moon has already crossed.

Possibilities under this condition. The 27 nakshatras are mapped to the 9 grahas in a repeating 9-pattern (so each planet owns 3 nakshatras): Ketu owns Ashwini, Magha, Mula; Venus owns Bharani, Purva-Phalguni, Purva-Ashadha; Sun owns Krittika, Uttara-Phalguni, Uttara-Ashadha; Moon owns Rohini, Hasta, Shravana; Mars owns Mrigashira, Chitra, Dhanishtha; Rahu owns Ardra, Swati, Shatabhisha; Jupiter owns Punarvasu, Vishakha, Purva-Bhadrapada; Saturn owns Pushya, Anuradha, Uttara-Bhadrapada; Mercury owns Ashlesha, Jyeshtha, Revati. The Moon's nakshatra at birth identifies the birth-MD lord. The balance of the birth-MD — the years left to live in that MD after birth — is calculated by a single proportionality rule: Balance = (Total years of birth-MD) × (degrees remaining in the nakshatra) ÷ (full nakshatra arc of 13°20′). So if the Moon is exactly at the start of a nakshatra, the entire MD remains; if exactly at the end, zero remains and the next MD begins at birth; if at the midpoint, exactly half the MD remains. The balance is then added to the birth-date to get the date the second MD begins. From that anchor, every subsequent MD-boundary is computed forward by simply adding the year-count of each planet in sequence. The same rule, applied recursively, generates AD boundaries (each AD = parent-MD-years × AD-lord-years ÷ 120), PD boundaries (PD = parent-AD-days × PD-lord-years ÷ 120), and onwards into SD and PrnD levels. Astrological software automates all of this; what the reader must understand is the rule, because the rule is what makes the resulting timeline auditable.

Strong possibility for this chart. For this chart, the Moon at birth sits at 2° Aquarius, which falls inside the nakshatra Dhanishtha (Dhanishtha spans 23°20′ Capricorn to 6°40′ Aquarius). Dhanishtha is owned by Mars in the Vimshottari mapping. Therefore the birth-MD is Mars, with total years 7. The balance-calculation: Dhanishtha ends at 6°40′ Aquarius; the Moon is at 2° Aquarius; so the degrees remaining in the nakshatra are 6°40′ − 2°00′ = 4°40′ = 4.667°. The full nakshatra arc = 13°20′ = 13.333°. Balance = 7 × 4.667 ÷ 13.333 = 7 × 0.350 = 2.45 years ≈ 2 years 5 months 12 days. Different astrological software produces minor variants (most commonly 2y 4m 19d versus 2y 5m 12d, depending on how precisely the software computes nakshatra-fraction). 18.5 The Five-Level Subdivision — MD · AD · PD · SD · PrnD१८.५ पाच-स्तरीय उपविभागणी — MD · AD · PD · SD · PrnD

Condition / state line. Each Mahadasha is recursively subdivided into nested sub-periods at four further levels — Antardasha (AD), Pratyantardasha (PD), Sookshma (SD), Prana (PrnD) — using a single recursive proportionality rule that converts the parent period's duration into the child period's duration by the child-lord's year-fraction of 120.

Possibilities under this condition. The five-level Vimshottari hierarchy: (1) Mahadasha (MD) — the "season" — 6 to 20 years long, named by the lord of the period (e.g. Jupiter MD). (2) Antardasha (AD) / Bhukti — the sub-period within an MD; each MD contains exactly nine ADs, one for each graha, in the Vimshottari sequence starting from the MD-lord itself. AD length: parent-MD-years × AD-lord-years ÷ 120, converted to months. Example: in the 16-year Jupiter-MD, Jupiter's own Antardasha = (16 × 16 ÷ 120) × 12 = 25.6 months ≈ 2y 1.6m; Saturn's Antardasha in Jupiter-MD = (16 × 19 ÷ 120) × 12 = 30.4 months ≈ 2y 6.4m; Venus's Antardasha in Jupiter-MD = (16 × 20 ÷ 120) × 12 = 32 months ≈ 2y 8m exactly. The 9 AD lengths within one MD always sum to the MD's total length (since 7+20+6+10+7+18+16+19+17 = 120 and any MD-years × 120 ÷ 120 = MD-years). (3) Pratyantardasha (PD) — the sub-sub-period within an AD; each AD contains nine PDs, in the Vimshottari sequence starting from the AD-lord. PD length: parent-AD-days × PD-lord-years ÷ 120, in days. PDs range from a few weeks to a few months — the level at which most actual life-events fire with date-precision useful for practical prediction. (4) Sookshma (SD) — the sub-sub-sub-period within a PD; nine SDs per PD, lengths in days to a couple of weeks. (5) Prana (PrnD) — the deepest sub-period commonly used; nine PrnDs per SD, lengths in hours to a couple of days. Some classical texts go even deeper (Deha, Sukshma-Prana) but five levels are practically sufficient. The recursive rule: child-period-duration = parent-period-duration × child-lord-years ÷ 120. The same rule applies at every level, which means once you know any one period-boundary, you can mechanically derive every nested boundary inside and around it. Astrological software does all this; the value of understanding the rule is that you can audit a printed dasha-table and catch off-by-one errors that software occasionally produces at decade boundaries.

Worked example: Jupiter-MD total = 16 years (5840 days approx); within it, the Jupiter-Venus AD = (16 × 20 ÷ 120) × 12 = 32 months running approximately April 2025 to December 2027. Within Ju-Ve AD, the 9 PDs in Vimshottari sequence starting from Venus run roughly: Venus-Venus (~5.3 months), Venus-Sun (~1.6 months), Venus-Moon (~2.7 months), Venus-Mars (~1.9 months), Venus-Rahu (~4.8 months), Venus-Jupiter (~4.3 months), Venus-Saturn (~5.1 months), Venus-Mercury (~4.5 months), Venus-Ketu (~1.9 months). The current PD on 15 May 2026 falls inside Venus-Moon roughly (computed from the recursive rule against the Apr-2025 anchor). At PD level, the firing-window for any specific event narrows to a couple of months — for marriage-fixing or job-fixing predictions, PD-level is the practical resolution. SD and PrnD are used mainly in muhurta (electional astrology) work and rarely matter for life-event prediction. Practically: the four-level chain MD → AD → PD → and-occasionally-SD is the standard depth this book uses; PrnD is mentioned for completeness only.

Overall Conclusion. The five-level Vimshottari hierarchy (MD/AD/PD/SD/PrnD) is generated by one recursive rule — child = parent × child-lord-years ÷ 120 — and gives finer-and-finer date-precision; for this chart the practical-prediction depth is MD-AD-PD, with the current PD ≈ Venus-Moon inside Ju-Ve AD inside Jupiter MD on 15 May 2026.

शक्यता. पाच-स्तरीय विंशोत्तरी श्रेणी: (१) महादशा (MD) — "ऋतू" — ६ ते २० वर्षे, स्वामीच्या नावाने ओळख (उदा. गुरू MD). (२) अंतर्दशा (AD) / भुक्ती — MD मधला उप-कालखंड; प्रत्येक MD मध्ये नेमक्या ९ AD, MD-स्वामीपासून सुरू होणार्‍या विंशोत्तरी क्रमाने. (३) प्रत्यंतर्दशा (PD) — AD मधला उप-उप-कालखंड; प्रत्येक AD मध्ये ९ PD, AD-स्वामीपासून सुरू होणार्‍या विंशोत्तरी क्रमाने. PD लांबी: पालक-AD-दिवस × PD-स्वामी-वर्षे ÷ १२०, दिवसांत. PD काही आठवडे ते काही महिने — व्यावहारिक भाकीतासाठी तारीख-अचूक स्तर. (४) सूक्ष्म (SD) — PD मधला उप-उप-उप-कालखंड; प्रत्येक PD मध्ये ९ SD, दिवस ते दोन आठवडे. (५) प्राण (PrnD) — सामान्यपणे वापरला जाणारा सर्वात-खोल उप-कालखंड; प्रत्येक SD मध्ये ९ PrnD, तास ते दोन दिवस. काही शास्त्रीय ग्रंथ अधिक खोल जातात (देह, सूक्ष्म-प्राण) पण व्यावहारिक हेतूसाठी पाच स्तर पुरेसे. पुनरावृत्त-नियम: मुलगा-कालावधी = पालक-कालावधी × मुलगा-स्वामी-वर्षे ÷ १२०. हाच नियम प्रत्येक स्तरावर लागू, म्हणजे कोणताही एक कालखंड-सीमा माहित असल्यास सर्व नेस्टेड सीमा यंत्रवत् सिद्ध. सॉफ्टवेअर हे सर्व करते; नियम समजण्याचा फायदा = छापलेला दशा-तक्ता तपासता येतो व सॉफ्टवेअर कधीकधी दशक-सीमांवर करते ती ऑफ-बाय-वन चुक पकडता येते.

या कुंडलीत प्रत्यक्ष. पाच-स्तरीय उपविभागणी गुरू-MD (२०१७-२०३३) ला सध्याच्या-कालखंड-विश्लेषणासाठी पुनरावृत्त-लागू. कार्य-उदाहरण: गुरू-MD एकूण = १६ वर्षे (~५८४० दिवस); त्यात गुरू-शुक्र AD = (१६ × २० ÷ १२०) × १२ = ३२ महिने ≈ एप्रिल २०२५ ते डिसेंबर २०२७. गुरू-शुक्र AD मध्ये ९ PD शुक्रापासून सुरू: शुक्र-शुक्र (~५.३ महिने), शुक्र-सूर्य (~१.६ महिने), शुक्र-चंद्र (~२.७ महिने), शुक्र-मंगळ (~१.९ महिने), शुक्र-राहू (~४.८ महिने), शुक्र-गुरू (~४.३ महिने), शुक्र-शनी (~५.१ महिने), शुक्र-बुध (~४.५ महिने), शुक्र-केतू (~१.९ महिने). १५ मे २०२६ रोजी सध्याची PD अंदाजे शुक्र-चंद्र (एप्रिल-२०२५ ठाण्यावरून पुनरावृत्त-नियमाने मोजली). 18.7 The Six-Axis Read of Any Dasha Lord१८.७ कोणत्याही दशा-स्वामीचे सहा-अक्षांचे वाचन

Condition / state line. To read what any Mahadasha or Antardasha will deliver, the dasha-lord is examined along six independent axes — its natal sign-and-house, its dignity, its aspects-and-yutis, its nakshatra-and-nakshatra-dispositor, its house-rulership, and its yoga-participation; the verdict is the combined synthesis of these six readings, weighted by their relative strength.

Possibilities under this condition. The six axes in order: (1) Sign and house. Which rashi does the lord sit in, and which bhava? The sign gives the lord's "tone" (e.g. Jupiter in earthy practical Capricorn behaves very differently from Jupiter in fiery teaching-temperament Sagittarius); the house gives the lord's "domain of operation" (e.g. Jupiter in 6H pours its blessing into the 6th-house themes of service, health, debt-clearance, victory-over-enemies, daily-discipline). (2) Dignity. Is the lord exalted, in own sign, in friend's sign, in neutral, in enemy, or in debilitation? Is it combust (within ~8° of the Sun)? Is it retrograde? Are any of the special-strength conditions (vargottama in D-9, pushkar-amsha, etc.) met? Dignity determines how cleanly the lord can deliver its potential. (3) Aspects and yutis. Which planets aspect this lord (and by what aspect — 7th-standard, 4/8 of Mars, 5/9 of Jupiter, 3/10 of Saturn), and which planets conjoin it in the same sign? Benefic aspects/yutis amplify the lord's positive potential; malefic aspects/yutis can corrupt or constrain it. (4) Nakshatra and its dispositor. Which nakshatra does the lord sit in, and where is that nakshatra's owner placed in the chart? A planet inherits significantly from its nakshatra-lord — the nakshatra-lord's house and sign flavour the planet's expression. (5) House-rulership. Which houses does this lord own in this chart (by sign-mapping from the Lagna)? In every Mahadasha or Antardasha, the houses owned by the lord activate strongly — events related to those houses are likely. (6) Yoga-participation. Which named yogas does this lord participate in (Raja, Dhana, Vipareeta, Pancha-Mahapurusha, Neecha-Bhanga, Chandra-yogas, etc., per ch16)? Yogas deliver primarily in the MD/AD of their participating planets, so the dasha-lord's yoga-list is a direct map of what the period will deliver. The combined six-axis read is then synthesised: each axis contributes a partial reading; the dominant axis often wins, but all six must be consulted to avoid one-dimensional predictions.

Strong possibility for this chart. Worked example: the six-axis read of Jupiter (the current MD-lord). Axis 1 (sign and house): Sagittarius (own, fiery teaching sign), 6H (service, health, debt-clearance, victory-over-enemies). Axis 4 (nakshatra and dispositor): Jupiter in Mula nakshatra (Ketu-ruled). Mula = root-cutting, deep-research, going-to-the-source. Ketu is placed in 9H Pisces in own-nakshatra Uttara-Bhadrapada (per ch14) — so Jupiter inherits Ketu-flavoured root-research-energy with a dharma-9H anchor. Axis 5 (house-rulership): Jupiter rules 6H (Sagittarius) and 9H (Pisces) — service-and-dharma both. या कुंडलीत प्रत्यक्ष. कार्य-उदाहरण: गुरू चे सहा-अक्ष-वाचन (सध्याचा MD-स्वामी). अक्ष १ (राशी-भाव): धनू (स्व, अग्नि-शिकवण-राशी), ६H (सेवा, आरोग्य, ऋण-मुक्ती, शत्रू-विजय). गुरू अडथळ्यांच्या भावात पण स्व-राशीत — अडथळे विजयांत रूपांतरित. अक्ष २ (दिग्बल): स्व-राशी = कमाल स्व-बल; जन्मवेळी अस्तंगत नाही, वक्र नाही; D-9 मध्ये पुष्कर-अंश — एकूण पूर्ण-सशक्त. अक्ष ३ (दृष्टी-युती): गुरूच्या तीन विशेष दृष्टी (५वी १०H मेषावर — करिअर; ७वी १२H मिथुनावर — परदेश/खर्च; ९वी २H सिंहावर — धन). इतर कोणत्या प्रमुख ग्रहाची या कुंडलीच्या भूमितीत ६H धनूवर दृष्टी नाही — म्हणून गुरू दृष्टी-अप्रभावी. युती नाही (गुरू ६व्यात एकटा). अक्ष ४ (नक्षत्र-स्वामी): गुरू मूळ नक्षत्रात (केतू-स्वामी). मूळ = मूळ-छेद, खोल-संशोधन, स्रोताकडे जाणे. केतू ९H मीनेत स्व-नक्षत्र उत्तर-भाद्रपदेत (अध्याय १४) — म्हणून गुरू केतू-स्वादाची मूळ-संशोधन-ऊर्जा धर्म-९H ठाण्यासह वारसा घेतो. अक्ष ५ (भाव-स्वामित्व): गुरू ६H (धनू) व ९H (मीन) स्वामी — सेवा व धर्म दोन्ही. अक्ष ६ (योग-सहभाग): गुरू हर्ष-योगात (पूर्ण, §१६.५), संन्यास-योगात ९L च्या भाग्य-ठाण्यात (§१६.८, अध्याय १४), धन-योगांत ९वी दृष्टी २H वर, राज-योगांत ५L-९L मंगळाशी परस्पर दृष्टी, आंशिक गजकेसरी (गुरू चंद्रापासून ११व्यात, केंद्रात नाही). संयुक्त सहा-अक्ष-वाचन: गुरू = कुंडलीचा सर्वात-घनदाट एकल योग-केंद्र, स्व-राशीत पूर्ण-सशक्त, तीन इतर भावांवर दृष्टी (१०H करिअर, २H धन, १२H परदेश/खर्च), नक्षत्राद्वारे केतू-मूळ खोल-संशोधन-स्वाद वारसा, व कुंडलीच्या दोन सर्वात-बलवान विषय-भावांचे स्वामी (६ व ९). म्हणून गुरू MD फळ: धर्म-ठाण-बद्ध करिअर-स्थैर्य, सेवा-व-शिकवण-सक्रिय, नैतिक २H मार्गांनी धन-प्रवाह, धर्म-९H विषयांचे शिखर (अध्याय २१ नुसार ७H विवाहही ९H भाग्याद्वारे ओढले), १२H दृष्टी व मूळ नक्षत्राद्वारे परदेश-किंवा-संशोधन-विषय. हे सहा-अक्ष-वाचन अध्याय २१ च्या पूर्ण गुरू-MD विश्लेषणाचा कणा.

निष्कर्ष. प्रत्येक दशा-स्वामी सहा अक्षांवर वाचला जातो (राशी/भाव, दिग्बल, दृष्टी/युती, नक्षत्र/स्वामी, भाव-स्वामित्व, योग-सहभाग); गुरू (सध्याचा MD) ला लागू केल्यावर संश्लेषण = पूर्ण-सशक्त-स्व-राशी-६H-मूळ-केतू-स्वामी-हर्ष-राज-धन-केंद्र — अध्याय १९-२३ हाच सहा-अक्ष-नियम प्रत्येक इतर MD ला लागू करतात.

18.8 The Transit Overlay — Dasha as Potential, Gochar as Firing Window१८.८ गोचर-आच्छादन — दशा शक्यता, गोचर सक्रिय-खिडकी

Condition / state line. A Mahadasha or Antardasha names what a period can deliver — its thematic potential — but the actual dating of events within that period is determined by gochar (transit) of the slow-moving planets (Jupiter, Saturn, Rahu/Ketu, sometimes Mars) over the natal points of the dasha-lord and its associated houses; dasha sets the canvas, gochar paints the moment.

Possibilities under this condition. The transit-overlay rules a working astrologer applies during any dasha analysis: (1) Transit of the MD-lord over its own natal position. When the gochar of the MD-lord crosses or aspects its own natal house/sign, the MD's themes peak. Example: Jupiter MD with transit-Jupiter returning to natal Sagittarius (6H) once every ~12 years — this is a Jupiter-return window and amplifies the MD's signature. (2) Transit of the AD-lord over the houses it activates. During Ju-Ve AD, transit-Venus over 7H Capricorn (marriage house) is a marriage-fixing window; transit-Venus over 2H Leo (wealth-house) is a wealth-event window. (3) Saturn's gochar over Lagna, Moon, or natal-MD-lord position. Saturn is the slowest visible planet and its transit-positions carry maximum "anchoring" weight. The Sade-Sati window (Saturn transiting 12H-1H-2H from Moon, ~7.5 years) is universally treated as a major life-restructure window regardless of which MD is running — discussed in detail in ch25. (4) Jupiter's gochar over Lagna or natal-MD-lord position. Jupiter transits each sign for ~12-13 months; its passage over the natal-position of the running MD-lord is a confirmation-trigger for events the dasha potentially carries. (5) Rahu-Ketu transit over natal axis. The lunar nodes complete one zodiac-cycle every ~18 years (retrograde). When they transit over the natal Rahu-Ketu axis (the "Rahu/Ketu return") or over the natal Sun/Moon/Lagna, they activate karmic-event windows. (6) Eclipses on natal points. Solar and lunar eclipses falling within ~3° of a natal planet or house-cusp are universally treated as event-trigger points; they accelerate whatever the MD/AD was already preparing. (7) Mars transit over natal Mars or 8H. Mars-transit is faster (~45 days per sign) but its passage over key natal points fires accident/conflict/sudden-event windows. (8) Mercury, Venus, Sun transits. Faster planets refine the timing to the week or day level once the slower-planet anchors are set. The classical principle: no event without a transit trigger. A dasha can hold a yoga's potential for years, but the actual event-date almost always coincides with one of these gochar triggers crossing a natal hot-spot.

अटी-स्थिती. महादशा किंवा अंतर्दशा काय देऊ शकते हे नाव देते — विषयांची शक्यता — पण त्या कालखंडात घटनांची नेमकी तारीख गोचर (मंद-गती ग्रह — गुरू, शनी, राहू/केतू, कधी मंगळ) दशा-स्वामीच्या व त्याच्या संबंधित भावांच्या जन्म-स्थानांवरून जात असताना ठरते; दशा कॅनव्हास टाकते, गोचर क्षण रंगवते.

शक्यता. कोणत्याही दशा-विश्लेषणादरम्यान कार्यरत-ज्योतिषी लागू करणारे गोचर-आच्छादन-नियम: (१) MD-स्वामीचे स्वतःच्या जन्म-स्थानावरून गोचर. MD-स्वामीचे गोचर त्याच्या स्वतःच्या जन्म-भाव/राशीवरून जाते किंवा दृष्टी देते तेव्हा MD ची विषय-वस्तू शिखरावर. उदा: गुरू MD मध्ये गोचर-गुरू दर ~१२ वर्षांनी एकदा जन्म-धनू (६H) कडे परतते — गुरू-परतीची खिडकी, MD-स्वाक्षरी वर्धन. (२) AD-स्वामीचे त्याने सक्रिय केलेल्या भावांवरून गोचर. गुरू-शुक्र AD मध्ये गोचर-शुक्र ७H मकरावरून (विवाह-भाव) = विवाह-निश्चिती खिडकी; गोचर-शुक्र २H सिंहावरून (धन-भाव) = धन-घटना खिडकी. (३) शनीचे लग्न, चंद्र, किंवा जन्म-MD-स्वामीच्या स्थानावरून गोचर. शनी = सर्वात-मंद दृश्य ग्रह व त्याची गोचर-स्थिती सर्वाधिक "ठाण" वजन. साडे-साती खिडकी (चंद्रापासून १२H-१H-२H वरून शनी, ~७.५ वर्षे) कोणतीही MD चालू असो प्रमुख जीवन-पुनर्रचना खिडकी म्हणून सर्वत्र मानली — अध्याय २५ मध्ये सविस्तर. (४) गुरूचे लग्न किंवा जन्म-MD-स्वामीच्या स्थानावरून गोचर. गुरू प्रत्येक राशीत ~१२-१३ महिने; चालू असलेल्या MD-स्वामीच्या जन्म-स्थानावरून त्याचे गमन = त्या दशा-संभाव्य-घटनांसाठी पुष्टी-ट्रिगर. (५) राहू-केतूचे जन्म-अक्षावरून गोचर. चंद्र-नोड दर ~१८ वर्षांनी एक राशी-चक्र पूर्ण करतात (वक्र). जन्म-राहू-केतू अक्षावर ("राहू/केतू परती") किंवा जन्म-सूर्य/चंद्र/लग्नावरून गोचर तेव्हा कर्म-घटना-खिडक्या सक्रिय. (६) ग्रहण जन्म-स्थानांवर. सूर्य-चंद्र-ग्रहण जन्म-ग्रह किंवा भाव-कुंजीच्या ~३° च्या आत आल्यास सर्वत्र घटना-ट्रिगर मानले; MD/AD जे आधीच तयार करत होते त्याला गती. (७) मंगळाचे जन्म-मंगळ किंवा ८H वरून गोचर. मंगळ-गोचर अधिक वेगवान (~४५ दिवस प्रति राशी) पण मुख्य जन्म-स्थानांवरून त्याचे गमन अपघात/संघर्ष/अचानक-घटना खिडक्या उघडते. (८) बुध, शुक्र, सूर्य गोचर. अधिक-वेगवान ग्रह आठवडा-किंवा-दिवस पातळीवर वेळ-शुद्धीकरण, मंद-ग्रह ठाणे सेट झाल्यावर. शास्त्रीय तत्त्व: गोचर-ट्रिगरशिवाय घटना नाही. दशा एका योगाची शक्यता वर्षानुवर्षे धरून ठेवू शकते, पण नेमकी घटना-तारीख जवळपास नेहमी या गोचर-ट्रिगरांपैकी एका जन्म-गरम-बिंदूवरून जाण्याशी जुळते.

या कुंडलीत प्रत्यक्ष. कार्य-उदाहरण: सध्याची गुरू MD (२०१७-२०३३) मुख्य गोचर-खिडक्यांसह आच्छादित. 18.9 Triangulation for Event-Timing — Three Confirmations Rule१८.९ घटना-वेळेसाठी त्रिकोणीकरण — तीन-पुष्टी नियम

Condition / state line. Classical Jyotish requires three independent confirmations before naming a date for any major life-event — (1) the running MD's lord must be thematically connected to the event-house, (2) the running AD's lord must independently confirm the same event-house, and (3) a slow-planet transit (gochar) must be activating that same house or one of its lords; only when all three align does the event fire reliably within a narrow window.

Possibilities under this condition. The three-confirmation rule is the practical safeguard that distinguishes responsible Jyotish prediction from one-axis guesswork. The three confirmations: (1) MD-lord ↔ event-house link. The Mahadasha-lord is examined for any connection to the house signifying the event: ownership of the house, occupation of the house, aspect upon the house, mutual aspect with the house-lord, sign-exchange with the house-lord. Any of these counts as a linkage; multiple linkages strengthen the case. (2) AD-lord ↔ event-house link. The same examination is repeated for the Antardasha-lord. Both lords confirming is much stronger than only one confirming. The classical rule: an event the MD-lord and the AD-lord both confirm is "ready to fire"; an event only one of them confirms is "potential but not certain"; an event neither confirms is "extremely unlikely this period". (3) Gochar trigger. A slow-planet transit (Jupiter, Saturn, or Rahu/Ketu) over the event-house, its lord's natal position, or the MD/AD-lord's natal position, supplies the final ignition. Without a gochar trigger, even a dasha-doubly-confirmed event tends to "hover" — the conditions are right, but the spark is missing. With a gochar trigger added, the event fires within typically 3-6 months. Additional refinements: (a) divisional-chart confirmation — the relevant D-chart (D-9 for marriage, D-10 for career, D-7 for children, etc.) should also show the event-readiness; (b) eclipse acceleration — an eclipse falling on a relevant point inside the dasha-window can shorten the typical 3-6 month firing-delay to a couple of weeks; (c) Mars/Mercury transit fine-tuning — faster-planet transits narrow the firing-window from months to weeks once the slow-planet anchors are in place. The framing principle: Jyotish does not predict "X will happen on date Y" with single-axis certainty; it predicts "X has a high probability of firing within window Y-to-Z, because three independent indicators all point to the same window". The honest framing of any prediction is always a window, not a date.

Jupiter also aspects 2H by 9th-aspect, and 2H signifies family/kutumba expansion (a marriage-adjacent house). So Jupiter MD links to marriage indirectly but solidly through karaka-status, 2H-aspect, and 9H-rulership. Confirmation 2 (AD-lord Venus ↔ 7H/marriage-house). Venus is the natural karaka of marriage and relationships. Venus is placed in 2H Leo conjunct Mars (the marriage-and-spouse-vitality combination, per ch11 §11.17). अटी-स्थिती. शास्त्रीय ज्योतिष कोणत्याही मोठ्या जीवन-घटनेची तारीख सांगण्यापूर्वी तीन स्वतंत्र पुष्टी मागते — (१) चालू MD चा स्वामी घटना-भावाशी विषयाने जोडलेला असावा, (२) चालू AD चा स्वामी त्याच घटना-भावाची स्वतंत्र पुष्टी द्यावा, व (३) मंद-ग्रह-गोचर त्याच भावाला किंवा त्याच्या एका स्वामीला सक्रिय करत असावे; तिन्ही जुळल्यावरच घटना संकुचित-खिडकीत विश्वासाने घडते.

शक्यता. तीन-पुष्टी नियम = जबाबदार ज्योतिष-भाकीताला एक-अक्षाच्या अंदाजांपासून वेगळे करणारा व्यावहारिक संरक्षक. तीन पुष्टी: (१) MD-स्वामी ↔ घटना-भाव संबंध. महादशेच्या स्वामीची घटना-दर्शक भावाशी कोणत्याही जोडणीसाठी तपासणी: भावाचे स्वामित्व, भावात उपस्थिती, भावावर दृष्टी, भाव-स्वामीशी परस्पर दृष्टी, भाव-स्वामीशी राशी-विनिमय. यापैकी कोणतेही जोडणी म्हणून मोजले जाते; अनेक जोडण्या केस मजबूत करतात. (२) AD-स्वामी ↔ घटना-भाव संबंध. अंतर्दशेच्या स्वामीसाठी तीच तपासणी पुन्हा. दोन्ही स्वामी पुष्टी देणे एकाने देण्यापेक्षा खूप बलवान. शास्त्रीय नियम: MD-स्वामी व AD-स्वामी दोघेही पुष्टी देणारी घटना "फायर-तयार"; फक्त एक पुष्टी देणारी "शक्य पण निश्चित नाही"; कोणीच पुष्टी न देणारी "या कालखंडात अत्यंत-असंभाव्य". (३) गोचर-ट्रिगर. मंद-ग्रह-गोचर (गुरू, शनी, किंवा राहू/केतू) घटना-भावावरून, त्याच्या स्वामीच्या जन्म-स्थानावरून, किंवा MD/AD-स्वामीच्या जन्म-स्थानावरून जात असता अंतिम प्रज्वलन. गोचर-ट्रिगरशिवाय दशा-दुहेरी-पुष्टी असलेली घटनाही "घोटाळते" — परिस्थिती योग्य पण ठिणगी गहाळ. गोचर-ट्रिगर जोडल्यावर घटना सहसा ३-६ महिन्यांत फायर. अतिरिक्त सूक्ष्मीकरण: (अ) अंश-कुंडली पुष्टी — संबंधित D-कुंडली (विवाहासाठी D-9, करिअरसाठी D-10, अपत्यासाठी D-7, इत्यादी) घटना-तयारी दर्शवायला हवी; (ब) ग्रहण-गती — दशा-खिडकीच्या आत संबंधित बिंदूवर पडलेले ग्रहण नेहमीच्या ३-६ महिन्यांच्या फायर-विलंबाला काही आठवड्यांपर्यंत संक्षिप्त करते; (क) मंगळ/बुध-गोचर सूक्ष्म-ट्यून — मंद-ग्रह ठाणे सेट झाल्यावर वेगवान-ग्रह-गोचर फायर-खिडकी महिन्यांपासून आठवड्यांपर्यंत संकुचित. तत्त्व: ज्योतिष "X तारीख Y रोजी घडेल" अशा एक-अक्षाच्या निश्चिततेने भाकीत करत नाही; ते "X ची Y-ते-Z खिडकीत फायरची उच्च संभाव्यता, कारण तीन स्वतंत्र निर्देशक त्याच खिडकीकडे" अशी भाकीत करते. कोणत्याही भाकीताची प्रामाणिक मांडणी नेहमी खिडकी, तारीख नव्हे.

निष्कर्ष. तीन-पुष्टी नियम (MD-स्वामी + AD-स्वामी + गोचर सर्वच त्याच घटना-भावाकडे) = एक-अक्षाच्या अति-भाकीताविरुद्ध शास्त्रीय संरक्षक; नातीकाच्या विवाह-प्रश्नाला लागू केल्यावर खिडकी २०२६-मध्य ते २०२७-मध्य तिहेरी-पुष्टी (गुरू MD, गुरू-शुक्र AD, गोचर-गुरू लग्नावरून + साडे-साती टप्पा ३) — कुंडलीतील सर्वाधिक-संभाव्यता विवाह-फायर खिडकी.

(८) साडे-साती टप्पे: टप्पा १ = शनी मकरेत (चंद्रापासून १२H) जाने २०२० — जाने २०२२; टप्पा २ = शनी कुंभेत (१H) जाने २०२२ — मार्च २०२५; टप्पा ३ = शनी मीनेत (२H) मार्च २०२५ — मार्च २०२८ — सध्या टप्पा-३ च्या मध्यभागी, धर्म-व-पुनर्रचना टप्पा. (९) महानिधी-योगी शिखर: शनी-MD २०३३-२०५२ मध्ये, विशेषतः सुरुवातीची शनी-शनी AD (मे २०३३ — मे २०३६). वरचा नऊ-ओळी सारांश = कुंडलीची संपूर्ण विंशोत्तरी-बोटाची-छाप; अध्याय १९-२७ या छापातील एका-एका ओळीचे विस्तारीकरण.
