This is the final chapter of Part II. Chapters 7 through 14 walked the chart planet by planet: Cancer Lagna, Aquarius Moon, then each occupied house with the planets sitting in it. Chapter 15 now flips the lens. We walk the chart house by house — all twelve in sequence — and at every house we ask the same four questions: (1) what does the house signify, (2) where is its lord and how strong, (3) which planets occupy it, (4) which planets aspect it. The reading emerges from combining those four answers. By the end you will see how chart-reading actually works — not as isolated planet-and-sign pieces but as the integration that produces a coherent life-picture. Layer 1 (15.1–15.14): the 4-step house-reading methodology (15.1), twelve houses in sequence with chart-specific readings (15.2–15.13), and the Bhava-Bala summary identifying the chart's three strongest and three weakest houses (15.14), every section 400+EN/400+MR. Layer 2 (15.15): one concentrated synthesis paragraph integrating all prior chart-readings into a single life-picture with timeline highlights, signature yogas, and consultation closer. 15.16: recap covering all 14 chart-houses and the life-arc bridging into Part III.

हा भाग दुसर्‍याचा शेवटचा अध्याय. अध्याय ७ ते १४ मध्ये आपण कुंडली ग्रहा-ग्रहाने चालत आलो: कर्क लग्न, कुंभ चंद्र, मग प्रत्येक भरलेला भाव त्यातील ग्रहांसह. अध्याय १५ मध्ये दृष्टीकोन उलटतो. आता कुंडली भावा-भावाने चालू — सर्व बारा भाव क्रमाने — आणि प्रत्येक भावात तेच चार प्रश्न विचारू: (१) त्या भावाचे कारकत्व काय, (२) त्याचा स्वामी कुठे व किती बलवान, (३) त्यात कोणते ग्रह बसले, (४) त्यावर कोणत्या ग्रहांची दृष्टी पडते. या चार उत्तरांच्या एकत्रीकरणातून भावाचे वाचन उमटते. अध्याय संपताना तुम्हाला कुंडली-वाचन प्रत्यक्षात कसे चालते हे दिसेल — विलग ग्रह-राशी-तुकडे म्हणून नव्हे, तर एकात्म जीवन-चित्र उत्पन्न करणारे एकत्रीकरण म्हणून. स्तर १ (१५.१–१५.१४): चार-पायरी भाव-वाचन-पद्धत (१५.१), क्रमाने बारा भाव त्या-त्या कुंडली-विशिष्ट वाचनासह (१५.२–१५.१३), आणि भावबल-सारांश तीन सर्वाधिक-बलवान व तीन सर्वाधिक-दुर्बल भावांची ओळख (१५.१४), प्रत्येक विभाग ४०० + EN / ४०० + MR. स्तर २ (१५.१५): सर्व पूर्व-वाचनांचे एक केंद्रित-संश्लेषण-परिच्छेद — टाइमलाइन, हस्ताक्षर-योग, समापन-वाक्य. १५.१६: चौदा कुंडली-भावांचा सारांश व भाग ३ मध्ये पूल.

15.1 The 4-Step House-Reading Rule — How to Read Any House in Any Chart१५.१ चार-पायरी भाव-वाचन-सूत्र — कोणत्याही कुंडलीतला कोणताही भाव कसा वाचावा

Every house in every chart is read by the same four-step method. Step 1: Lord-condition. Identify which planet rules the rashi on that house cusp. Then check that planet's sign-dignity (own, exalted, debilitated, friendly, enemy), house-position (which bhava it occupies), nakshatra (and pada), conjunctions, aspects received, and retrograde-status. A house is fundamentally as strong as its lord is. A 10th lord exalted in 11th makes the 10th house produce strong career-results even if the 10th house itself sits empty; a 7th lord debilitated and combust makes the 7th house struggle even if it has benefics in it. Step 2: Occupants. List the planets sitting inside the house. Each occupant colors the house with its own nature, dignity, and significations. Jupiter in the 6th house gives Harsha-Yoga-protection; debilitated Sun in the 4th gives childhood-friction-with-father; Mars and Venus together in the 2nd produce a sharp-and-sweet voice. Empty houses are not weak — they simply borrow their result from the lord (Step 1) and the aspects (Step 4); many full Maha-Raja-Yogas form in empty houses through aspect-and-lord-rules. Step 3: Aspects. List every planet that throws a direct or special aspect onto the house. The basic 7th-aspect operates for all planets; Mars adds the 4th and 8th, Jupiter adds the 5th and 9th, Saturn adds the 3rd and 10th, Rahu and Ketu add 5th-7th-9th in some schools. An aspect from Jupiter is the chart's strongest blessing on any house; an aspect from Saturn imposes discipline and delay but rewards endurance; aspects from Mars add energy and conflict-readiness; aspects from Rahu add unconventional-and-foreign coloring. Step 4: Nakshatra-and-pada of the cusp. The fine-grained "flavor" of the house comes from the nakshatra on its cusp — which gives the deity, the elemental quality, the pushkar-or-gandanta status, and the pada-sub-amsha. Two charts with the same rashi on the same house can read very differently because their cusp-nakshatras differ. Combine all four answers and the house's reading emerges. Apply this rule to every house, every chart, every time.

कोणत्याही कुंडलीतला कोणताही भाव एकाच चार-पायरी पद्धतीने वाचला जातो. पायरी १ — स्वामी-स्थिती: भाव-कुस्पाची राशी कोणत्या ग्रहाची ते ओळखा. मग त्या ग्रहाचे राशी-दिग्बल (स्व, उच्च, नीच, मित्र, शत्रू), भाव-स्थान (कोणता बहाव), नक्षत्र (आणि पाद), युती, मिळणाऱ्या दृष्टी, वक्र-गती तपासा. भाव मूलतः त्याचा स्वामी जितका बलवान तितकाच बलवान असतो. १०चा स्वामी ११ व्यात उच्च = १० रिकामा असला तरी कारकीर्द प्रबल; ७चा स्वामी नीच व अस्तंगत = ७ भरलेला असला तरी विवाह-संघर्ष. पायरी २ — उपस्थित ग्रह: भावात बसलेले ग्रह यादी करा. प्रत्येक उपस्थित ग्रह स्वतःच्या स्वभाव-दिग्बल-कारकत्वाने भावाला रंग देतो. ६व्यातील गुरू = हर्ष-योग-संरक्षण; ४व्यात नीच सूर्य = बालपणीचा वडील-संघर्ष; २र्‍यात मंगळ-शुक्र एकत्र = धारदार-गोड वाणी. रिकामा भाव दुर्बल नसतो — तो आपले फल फक्त स्वामीच्या (पायरी १) व दृष्टीच्या (पायरी ४) माध्यमातून घेतो; अनेक पूर्ण-महा-राजयोग रिकाम्या भावात स्वामी-दृष्टी-सूत्राने तयार होतात. पायरी ३ — दृष्टी: भावावर थेट किंवा विशेष दृष्टी टाकणारा प्रत्येक ग्रह यादी करा. ७वी मूल दृष्टी सर्व ग्रहांची; मंगळ ४-८ अधिकची, गुरू ५-९ अधिकची, शनी ३-१० अधिकची, राहू-केतू ५-७-९ काही शाखांनुसार. गुरूची दृष्टी = कुंडलीचा सर्वात मोठा शुभाशीर्वाद; शनीची दृष्टी = शिस्त व विलंब पण सहनशक्तीला बक्षीस; मंगळाची दृष्टी = ऊर्जा व संघर्ष-सज्जता; राहूची दृष्टी = अपारंपरिक व परदेशी रंग. पायरी ४ — कुस्प-नक्षत्र व पाद: भावाचा सूक्ष्म "स्वाद" त्याच्या कुस्पावरील नक्षत्रातून येतो — देवता, तत्त्व-गुण, पुष्कर-किंवा-गंडान्त स्थिती, पाद-उप-अंश. एकाच भावात एकच राशी असलेल्या दोन कुंडल्या त्यांच्या कुस्प-नक्षत्र भिन्न असल्याने फार वेगळे वाचन देतात. चारही उत्तरे एकत्र करा, भावाचे वाचन तयार होते. हे सूत्र प्रत्येक भाव, प्रत्येक कुंडली, प्रत्येक वेळी लागू.

15.2 House 1 (Lagna · Tanu-bhava) — Body · Identity · Vitality · Life-direction१५.२ भाव १ (लग्न · तनू-भाव) — शरीर · ओळख · चैतन्य · जीवन-दिशा

Generic significations. The 1st house is the chart's anchor. It governs the physical body and its complexion, the head and the face, the innate constitution and vitality (prana), the life-direction and overall life-arc, the early-childhood foundation, the persona projected to the world, and the soul-vehicle that carries the native through this lifetime. As the lagna (rising sign), it is also the reference-point from which all other twelve bhavas are counted — every other house is read relative to the lagna. Any reading begins here. This chart's 1st house. Cancer rises (4° Cancer per ch02). Lord: Moon. Moon is located in the 8th house (Aquarius, 2° Dhanishta Pada 3). Occupants of the 1st: none. Aspects on the 1st: by strict count, Mars (in 2H) does not aspect 1H by its special 4/7/8 aspects (those land on 5H, 8H, 9H from 2H — the 7th-from-2 aspect is 8H not 1H), Jupiter (in 6H) does not aspect 1H (its 5/7/9 aspects from 6 land on 10H, 12H, 2H), Saturn (in 9H) does not aspect 1H (its 3/7/10 aspects from 9 land on 11H, 3H, 6H), Ketu's nodal 5th aspect from 9H lands on 1H in schools that grant the nodes a 5th aspect. So the strongest functional inputs on the 1st are (a) the Moon's lagnesh-condition as it sits in the 8th, and (b) the soft 5th-aspect of Ketu from 9H. Reading. A Cancer Lagna gives a soft, watery, emotionally-attuned body; round-or-soft facial features; sensitive nervous system; high empathy as a baseline temperament. With Moon — the Lagnesh — sitting in 8H, the native's identity is bound to depth, transformation, and the hidden: the self redefines repeatedly through life-transitions, the inner life is more textured than the outer presentation, and the native's true centre is not visible to strangers. Pushkar-navamsha for the Moon (per ch08) protects emotional stability through that 8H placement, which would otherwise be a fraught position for the Lagnesh. Ketu's mild 5th-aspect from 9H adds a quiet detachment from purely physical concerns and inclines the native toward dharmic-philosophical questions from a young age. An empty 1st house with the lord far away is not weak — it makes the self-image flexible, the persona adaptable, and the life-direction discoverable rather than fixed. Cross-reference: detailed Cancer-Lagna analysis in ch07 §7.16; Moon-in-8H mechanics in ch10 §10.16.

सामान्य कारकत्व. पहिला भाव कुंडलीचा अंकुर. शरीर व रंग-रूप, डोके व चेहरा, सहज प्रकृति व चैतन्य (प्राण), जीवन-दिशा व जीवन-वक्र, बालपणीचा पाया, जगाला दाखवली जाणारी प्रतिमा, आणि या जन्मात आत्म्याला वाहून नेणारे "वाहन" — हे सर्व त्याच्या अधिकारात. लग्न म्हणून तो इतर बारा भावांचा संदर्भ-बिंदू देखील — सगळे भाव लग्नाशी सापेक्ष मोजले जातात. कोणतेही वाचन इथून सुरू होते. या कुंडलीचा पहिला भाव. कर्क लग्न (४° कर्क — अध्याय ०२). स्वामी: चंद्र. चंद्र ८व्या भावात (कुंभ, २° धनिष्ठा पाद ३). उपस्थित ग्रह: कोणी नाही. १व्यावर दृष्टी: काटेकोर मोजणीने मंगळ (२व्यातून) १व्यावर येत नाही (४/७/८ दृष्टी ५-८-९ वर पडतात), गुरू (६व्यातून) १ वर येत नाही (५/७/९ → १०-१२-२), शनी (९व्यातून) १ वर येत नाही (३/७/१० → ११-३-६); केतूची ९व्यापासूनची ५वी नोडल-दृष्टी काही शाखांनुसार १व्यावर येते. म्हणून १व्यावरील प्रबळ कार्यात्मक प्रवेश — (अ) ८व्यात बसलेला लग्नेश चंद्र व (ब) ९व्यातून केतूची सौम्य ५वी दृष्टी. वाचन. कर्क लग्न = मऊ, जलमय, भावनिक-संवेदनशील शरीर; गोल-मऊ चेहर्‍याची ठेवण; नाजूक नस-तंत्र; जन्मजात उच्च सहानुभूती. लग्नाधिपती चंद्र ८व्यात बसल्याने नातिक ओळख ↔ खोली, परिवर्तन, गुप्तता यांना बांधलेली: स्व-प्रतिमा प्रत्येक जीवन-संक्रमणात नव्याने रचली जाते, बाह्य-दर्शनापेक्षा अंतरंग अधिक खोल, अनोळख्यांना खरे केंद्र दिसत नाही. अध्याय ८ नुसार चंद्राचे पुष्कर-नवांश ८व्यातील भावनिक-स्थैर्याचे संरक्षण करते — अन्यथा हे लग्नेश-स्थान अधिक त्रासदायक झाले असते. केतूची ९व्यातून सौम्य ५वी दृष्टी = निव्वळ देहिक-चिंतांपासून शांत-विरक्ती व लहान वयापासूनच धर्म-तात्त्विक प्रश्नांकडे झुकाव. रिकामे लग्न व दूरचा स्वामी "दुर्बल" नाही — यामुळे स्व-प्रतिमा लवचिक, व्यक्तिमत्त्व जुळवून घेणारे, जीवन-दिशा ठरलेली नसून शोधण्याची ठरते. विस्तृत कर्क-लग्न वाचन अध्याय ७ §७.१६; ८व्यातील चंद्र अध्याय १० §१०.१६.

15.3 House 2 (Dhana-bhava) — Wealth · Voice · Family-of-Origin · Stored Value१५.३ भाव २ (धन-भाव) — संपत्ती · वाणी · जन्म-कुटुंब · संचित-मूल्य

Generic significations. The 2nd house governs accumulated wealth (as distinct from earned-income, which is 11th, or career-revenue, which is 10th), the voice and speech-quality, the face and eyes, food and what one consumes, the family-of-origin (the family one is born into, as distinct from the spouse-family which is 7th), the early-life education and values absorbed, and stored-value of every kind (savings, jewellery, property held). It is one of the two maraka (life-cutting) houses with the 7th, but this maraka-function activates only in difficult dasha periods; in normal life the 2nd is a wealth-and-resource house. The 2nd is also called kutumba, the family-house. This chart's 2nd house. Leo rises on the 2nd cusp. Lord: Sun. Sun is located in the 4th house (Libra, 5°, debilitated but with Neecha-Bhanga active). Occupants of the 2nd: Mars (1° Leo) and Venus (27° Leo, in own nakshatra Purva Phalguni). Aspects on the 2nd: Jupiter (in 6H) throws its special 9th-aspect onto 2H (6 + 9 - 1 = 14 mod 12 = 2 — yes, the 9th-aspect from 6 lands exactly on 2H). Saturn from 9H does not aspect 2H by its 3/7/10 special aspects (those land on 11, 3, 6). Mercury and Rahu in 3H do not aspect 2H. So the 2nd house receives: two occupants of significance (Mars the yogakaraka, Venus in own nakshatra), and Jupiter's 9th-aspect — the chart's strongest single-aspect benefic. Reading. This is the chart's most-loaded benefic-house. The mechanics: (a) Mars in 2H is the chart's yogakaraka (4L+9L for Cancer Lagna) anchoring wealth in karma-yoga effort; (b) Venus is the natural karaka of wealth, sitting in own nakshatra — extraordinary self-strength; (c) Sun the 2L returns its lordship to 4H but debilitation-with-Bhanga converts loss into late-life recovery; (d) Jupiter's 9th-aspect ethically channels the wealth — what is earned, is earned cleanly. The voice has a Mars-Venus duality: it can be sharp and decisive (Mars) when needed, sweet and persuasive (Venus) when needed; the native naturally code-switches between the two registers. Family-of-origin is emotionally central — Sun the father-karaka and 2L together place "family" at the chart's structural core. Learning Note · The 4-Step House-Reading Rule Applied Chart-by-Chart (Parashara, BPHS Chapter 27)

Rule: For any house in any chart, the reading is constructed by combining four answers — (1) where is the lord and how dignified, (2) which planets occupy it, (3) which planets aspect it, (4) what nakshatra-pada sits on its cusp. Each answer carries about a quarter of the house's reading; missing one of the four leaves the reading incomplete, weighting only one of the four (e.g. "Mars sits there so it's a Mars-house") flattens the reading into stereotype.

Applied to this chart, three worked examples: (a) 6th house — (1) lord Jupiter in 6H own sign = Harsha; (2) occupant Jupiter = same answer reinforced; (3) Saturn's 10th-aspect from 9H adds discipline; (4) cusp-nakshatra Mula or Purva-Ashadha depending on exact degree adds Ketu-flavored-roots or Venus-flavored-warmth. Combined reading: Harsha Yoga in its full classical-form, with Saturn-tempered service-life and Jupiter-style ethical-protection — the chart's single-strongest yoga. (b) 10th house — (1) lord Mars yogakaraka in 2H = career-produces-wealth; (2) no occupants = house is empty; (3) Sun's 7th-aspect + Jupiter's 5th-aspect = identity-fulfilment + ethical-blessing converge; (4) cusp-nakshatra in Aries probably Ashwini or Bharani — Ashwini gives healer-fast-mover quality, Bharani gives Venus-flavored-discipline-of-creation. Combined: empty-house can be one of the chart's strongest houses when the four answers align — exactly what happens here. (c) 4th house — (1) lord Venus in 2H own-nakshatra = home-wealth integration; (2) occupant Sun debilitated with Bhanga = childhood-friction-then-mid-life-recovery; (3) no direct aspects from outside; (4) cusp-nakshatra in Libra likely Swati (Rahu-ruled, mobility-flavored) or Vishakha — adds restlessness or goal-orientation. Combined: 4H is the chart's weakest house in absolute terms but operates via 4L+Bhanga to deliver eventually — not from house-occupation but from lord-mechanics. The four-step method does not change; only the proportions of the answer shift across houses.

Alternative possibilities: If only step 2 (occupants) is read, the chart looks like "many empty houses and a few cluttered ones" — unrepresentative. If only step 1 (lord) is read, the chart looks like "lords all over the place" without context. If only step 3 (aspects) is read, the chart looks like "Jupiter is busy and Saturn is structural" without anchor. The four-step rule is the integration. Apply it consistently to every chart, every house, every time — only then does the chart speak as a unified life-picture.
