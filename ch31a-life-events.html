Chapter 31a

Life-Event Yogas — Diagnostic Referenceजीवन-घटना योग — निदान-संदर्भ

When a client walks into an astrologer's room, the questions are almost always the same: "Will I divorce?", "Is there an affair coming?", "Will I win this court case?", "Will I lose my property?", "Should I go abroad?", "Why is my money slipping?", "Will my career hold?", "Can I finish my degree?", "Will we have children?", "Why is my marriage tense?" This chapter is the diagnostic catalog for those questions. Each life-event below is treated as a life-state that may or may not fire in a given chart, and each is read through a fixed four-step scaffold: (1) the event/state line that names the question, (2) the Astro-Possibilities layer that lists every classical combination capable of producing it, (3) the This-chart-specific drivers layer that names which MD/AD/PD is running, which natal yogas activate, and the gochar (Saturn, Jupiter, Rahu-Ketu, Mars) overlay, and (4) a one-paragraph Overall Conclusion that compresses the verdict. The negative cases — events whose yogas are absent — matter as much as the positive ones, because the astrologer who predicts an event whose yoga is not in the chart is reading the client's fear, not the kundli.

कोणताही जातक ज्योतिषासमोर येतो तेव्हा प्रश्न जवळपास ठरलेले असतात: "घटस्फोट होईल का?", "बाहेरचे संबंध येतायेत का?", "हे कोर्ट-केस जिंकेन का?", "मालमत्ता हातातून जाईल का?", "परदेशी जावे का?", "पैसा का घसरतोय?", "करिअर टिकेल का?", "शिक्षण पूर्ण होईल का?", "मूल होईल का?", "वैवाहिक तणाव का?" हा अध्याय त्या प्रश्नांचे निदान-संग्रहालय आहे. प्रत्येक जीवन-घटना जीवन-स्थिती म्हणून पाहिली जाते जी कुंडलीत सक्रिय असू शकते किंवा नसू शकते. प्रत्येक घटना चार-पायरी साच्याने: (१) घटना/स्थिती-ओळ, (२) शास्त्रीय शक्यता-स्तर (सर्व शास्त्रीय संयोग), (३) या-कुंडलीतले-विशिष्ट-चालक स्तर (कोणती MD/AD/PD चालू, कोणते जन्म-योग सक्रिय, गोचर-थर — शनी, गुरू, राहू-केतू, मंगळ), (४) एका-परिच्छेदाचा निष्कर्ष. नकारात्मक प्रकरणे — ज्या घटनांचे योग अनुपस्थित — सकारात्मक प्रकरणांइतकीच महत्त्वाची; कारण कुंडलीत योग नसताना घटना भाकित करणारा ज्योतिषी जातकाची भीती वाचतो, कुंडली नव्हे.

31a.1 Why Life-Events Triangulate the Chart३१अ.१ जीवन-घटना कुंडलीला त्रिकोणीकरण का करतात

Event / state line. The reason this chapter exists at all is that the chart is read in three independent registers — natal yogas (the permanent recipe), Vimshottari dasha (the time-clock that opens and closes those yogas), and gochar transits (the moment-by-moment activation key) — and a true diagnosis requires that all three converge on the same answer. A single register lies easily; three registers triangulating the same verdict almost never lie.

Astro Possibilities. The classical doctrine of triangulation (sometimes called tri-pramana in modern Jyotish writing) holds that any predicted event must satisfy three conditions: (1) the natal chart must carry the yoga for the event — i.e., the planet-house combination is structurally present; (2) the dasha currently running must include at least one planet from that yoga as MD-, AD-, or PD-lord; (3) the gochar at the moment must touch the yoga's house or planet — typically Jupiter's blessing, Saturn's pressure, or a node's activation. When all three fire, the event manifests strongly. When only two fire, the event manifests in muted or symbolic form. When only one fires, the event remains potential, not actual. The classical texts (Phaladeepika 26, Saravali 39, Brihat Jataka 12, Jaimini Upadesa-Sutras book 4) all repeat this same three-fold structure under different vocabularies. A second classical doctrine relevant here is the karaka-bhava-graha stack: every life event has a natural karaka (Venus for marriage, Jupiter for children, Mercury for learning), a bhava that hosts it (7H for marriage, 5H for children, 4H/5H for learning), and a graha actually placed there or aspecting. The diagnosis fires fully only when karaka, bhava, and graha all agree. When they disagree, the event splits into two milder forms or shifts in timing. A third doctrine — the cancellation-rule set — says that even a strong yoga can be cancelled by retrograde-debilitation, by combustion, or by a strong malefic aspect without benefic relief; conversely, a weak yoga can be redeemed by Neecha-Bhanga, by participation in a Raja-yoga, or by sub-period support from a friend-lord. These three doctrines together form the diagnostic grammar of every section that follows.

घटना/स्थिती-ओळ. हा अध्याय अस्तित्वात असण्याचे कारण: कुंडली तीन स्वतंत्र पटलांतून वाचली जाते — जन्म-योग (कायमस्वरूपी पाककृती), विंशोत्तरी दशा (योग उघडणारे-बंद करणारे काल-घड्याळ), व गोचर (क्षण-क्षणाचा सक्रियक — किल्ली). खरे निदान तेव्हाच जेव्हा तिन्ही पटले एकच उत्तर देतात. एकच पटल सहज खोटे बोलते; तिघे एकत्र त्रिकोणीकरण करताना क्वचितच खोटे बोलतात.

शास्त्रीय शक्यता. शास्त्रीय त्रि-प्रमाण-सिद्धांत (आधुनिक ज्योतिषात असे नाव) सांगतो: कोणत्याही भाकित घटनेला तीन अटी पूर्ण कराव्या लागतात: (१) जन्म-कुंडलीत त्या घटनेचा योग असला पाहिजे — ग्रह-भाव संयोजन रचनात्मक उपस्थित; (२) सध्या चालू असलेल्या दशेत त्या योगाच्या किमान एका ग्रहाचा MD-, AD-, किंवा PD-स्वामी म्हणून सहभाग; (३) त्या क्षणाला गोचर त्या योगाच्या भाव/ग्रहाला स्पर्श — सहसा गुरूचा आशीर्वाद, शनीचा दबाव, किंवा राहू-केतूची सक्रियता. तिन्ही पेटले की घटना प्रबळ; दोन पेटले की मंद/प्रतीकात्मक; एकच पेटले की केवळ शक्यता, घटना नव्हे. फलदीपिका, सारावली, बृहत्-जातक, जैमिनी-उपदेश-सूत्रे — सर्व ग्रंथ हीच रचना वेगवेगळ्या शब्दांत सांगतात. दुसरा शास्त्रीय सिद्धांत — कारक-भाव-ग्रह स्टॅक: प्रत्येक जीवन-घटनेचा एक नैसर्गिक कारक (विवाहाला शुक्र, मुलाला गुरू, शिक्षणाला बुध), यजमान-भाव (विवाहाला ७, मुलाला ५, शिक्षणाला ४/५), व प्रत्यक्ष-स्थित-ग्रह. तिन्ही सहमत असल्यासच निदान पूर्ण फल देते; न-सहमत असल्यास घटना दोन सौम्य रूपांत विभाजित किंवा वेळेत सरकते. तिसरा — रद्द-नियम: वक्र-नीच, अस्तंगत, शुभ-प्रति-दृष्टीशिवाय क्रूर-दृष्टीने प्रबळ योग रद्द होतो; उलट, नीचभंग, राजयोग-सहभाग, मित्र-स्वामी सब-कक्ष-समर्थनाने कमजोर योग पुनरुज्जीवित. हे तीन सिद्धांत खालील प्रत्येक विभागाची निदान-व्याकरण आहेत.

या-कुंडलीतले-विशिष्ट-चालक. या कुंडलीसाठी तिन्ही पटले आधीच ज्ञात. जन्म-योग-पटल: कर्क लग्न, मंगळ-शुक्र २व्यात, बुध+राहू ३व्यात, नीच सूर्य ४व्यात (काही वाचनांत केतूसह — पूर्वीच्या अध्यायांत स्थान-विसंगती नोंदलेली), चंद्र ८व्या धनिष्ठात, गुरू ६व्या धनूत (हर्ष), शनी ९व्या मीनेत उत्तरा-भाद्रपदात (संन्यास + महानिधी-योगी). दशा-पटल: मंगळ MD जन्म-१९९९, राहू MD १९९९-२०१७, गुरू MD सध्या २०१७-२०३३, शनी MD पुढे २०३३-२०५२. सध्याची AD = गुरू-शुक्र (फेब्रु २०२५ – ऑक्टो २०२७) — एकाच वेळी ९L-गुरू (स्व-राशी ६H हर्ष) व ४L-शुक्र (मंगळ-शुक्र २H धन-गुच्छ) सक्रिय करते — संपूर्ण १६-वर्षीय गुरू MD ची सर्वात घन AD. गोचर-पटल आज (मे २०२६): शनी मीनेत (जन्म-शनी-वर — साडेसाती तिसरा टप्पा, मकर-कुंभ-मीन तिढ्यातून बाहेर पडण्याचा टप्पा), गुरू वृषभातून मिथुनेकडे (४H तुळ-सूर्य-राहू-गुच्छाला दृष्टी व जन्म-१२H जवळ), राहू-केतू अक्ष मीन-कन्या (राहू जन्म-शनी-वर, केतू जन्म-बुधावर — अत्यंत सक्रिय नोडल टप्पा), मंगळ दर ६-८ आठवडे राशी बदलतो. तिन्ही पटले — जन्म, दशा, गोचर — सध्या सर्वाधिक सक्रिय; आयुष्यातील सर्वात घन त्रि-प्रमाण-संगम-खिडक्यांपैकी एक.

निष्कर्ष. २०२५-२०२७ खिडकी या कुंडलीतली सर्वात घन त्रि-प्रमाण-संगम-खिडकी — गुरू-शुक्र AD दशा-पटलाने गुरू+शुक्र योग उघडते, साडेसाती तिसरा टप्पा + राहू-शनीवरून ९H परिवर्तन-पटल उघडते, जन्म-हर्ष + मंगळ-शुक्र धन-योग + गुरूची ९वी दृष्टी २H वर रचनात्मक पाककृती पुरवते. खालील प्रत्येक निदान या त्रिकोणी पार्श्वभूमीवर वाचले जाते.

31a.2 Event-by-Event Walk३१अ.२ घटना-दर-घटना चालणी

Each is treated with the universal four-step scaffold — event/state line, Astro Possibilities (Layer 1, generic classical teaching), This-chart specific drivers (Layer 2, with dasha + transit overlay), and Overall Conclusion. 31a.2.1 Divorce / Separation३१अ.२.१ घटस्फोट / विभक्तता

Event / state line. The most-asked, most-feared question. Divorce = legal end of marriage; separation = de-facto living apart without legal closure. Both are read off the same yoga-cluster (7H, 7L, Venus, malefic load) but at different intensity thresholds.

Astro Possibilities. Classical rule: 7H (partnership) afflicted by multiple malefics (Mars, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu) AND 7L placed in dushthana (6/8/12) AND Venus (marriage karaka) afflicted by malefics. Strongest divorce-trigger is the combination 7L conjunct/aspected by Saturn while in a dushthana, with Venus simultaneously hit by Rahu or Ketu — three-way break of the partnership-significator stack. Secondary triggers: Mars in 7H (Kuja Dosha unaccompanied by cancellation), 7L combust by Sun, 7L retrograde-debilitated, Moon-Venus 6/8/12 axis afflicted, 7H lord exchange with 12L of expenditure or 6L of disputes (this becomes a separation-via-fight or separation-via-distance signature). Kuja Dosha — Mars in 1/2/4/7/8/12 from Lagna, Moon, or Venus — compounds whenever it stacks on the basic configuration but does not cause divorce alone; classical cancellation rules (Mars in own/exalted/friend's sign, Mars conjunct Venus, Mars aspected by Jupiter, etc.) routinely neutralize light-Manglik. The texts (Phaladeepika 17, Saravali 30) repeatedly emphasize that the divorce-yoga is a compound signature: a single Manglik or a single 7L-weakness rarely produces divorce; the chart must show three or four convergent afflictions before separation becomes a fated outcome rather than a passing-tension. Modern reading adds: in the dasha-period of a 6/8/12-stationed 7L during a malefic gochar over 7H, the structural-yoga (if present) delivers; without dasha and gochar, the yoga only stays latent. ~210 EN words.

This-chart specific drivers. The configuration in this kundli: 7H = Capricorn, empty (no malefic occupation). 7L = Saturn, placed in 9H Pisces in own-nakshatra Uttara Bhadrapada — a textbook dignified placement; 9H is a trikona (sacred angle), not a dushthana. Venus (marriage karaka) sits in 2H Leo with yogakaraka Mars at high degree (Venus 27° = near-pushyamsha strength). Mild Kuja Dosha exists from Mars in 2H from Lagna, but it is the lightest of the six Manglik positions and is multiply cancelled (Mars conjunct Venus, Mars in friend's sign Leo, Mars aspected by Jupiter's 9th-aspect from 6H). Across all three layers the divorce-yoga fails to fire: structurally absent, never activated by a Saturn-7L-dushthana dasha (Saturn is in trikona, not dushthana), and not currently triggered by gochar. The relevant dasha overlay is Jupiter MD (May 2017 – May 2033) with Ju-Ve AD running now (Feb 2025 – Oct 2027) — both Jupiter and Venus are marriage-friendly significators, which is the opposite of a separation window. Gochar overlay (May 2026): Saturn in Pisces transiting over natal Saturn in 9H = Sade-Sati Phase 3, which stresses the 9H-dharma axis but does not directly attack the 7H-partnership axis. Jupiter in Taurus/Gemini blesses through 11H-gains and approaches 12H — beneficial. Rahu-Ketu on the Pisces–Virgo axis = nodal activation of 9H and 3H, not 7H. No transit currently sits in or aspects 7H Capricorn from a malefic angle. Hypothetical-only activation window would be Sa-Ma AD (Nov 2045 – Dec 2046) when Mars-Saturn shastra-yoga could touch a marital-tension peak — but even there the chart's overall configuration shows accident/surgery risk rather than separation. ~250 EN words.

Overall Conclusion. The chart does NOT carry a divorce yoga across natal, dasha, or gochar layers. An astrologer telling this native "your marriage will end" is reading their anxiety, not the chart. Tensions yes (see §31a.2.11) — separation, no. Learning Notes / शिक्षण-नोंदी

(1) Triangulation beats single-register reading. Any chart-reading that uses only natal yogas (no dasha) or only dasha (no transit) is dangerously partial. The Cancer Lagna reader who sees Mars-Venus in 2H and predicts "wealth-life" without checking which MD is running will be right about the structure but wrong about the timing. Always read all three registers — natal recipe + dasha clock + transit key — before pronouncing.

(2) Negative cases teach as much as positive ones. When a chart lacks a divorce yoga, the lesson is "refuse to predict divorce." When it lacks a bankruptcy yoga, refuse to predict bankruptcy. The astrologer's first ethical duty is to NOT manufacture events the chart does not carry — that is what separates a counselor from a fear-monger.

(१) त्रिकोणीकरण एकल-थर वाचनापेक्षा श्रेष्ठ. केवळ जन्म-योग (दशा नाही) किंवा केवळ दशा (गोचर नाही) वापरून केलेले कोणतेही वाचन धोकादायकपणे अपूर्ण. कर्क-लग्न-वाचक जो २H मध्ये मंगळ-शुक्र पाहून "संपत्ति-जीवन" भाकित करतो पण कोणती MD चालू तपासत नाही — रचनेबाबत बरोबर पण वेळेबाबत चूक. नेहमी तिन्ही थर — जन्म-पाककृती + दशा-घड्याळ + गोचर-किल्ली — वाचा.
