# ज्योतिष ग्रंथ — The Kundli of Gitesh Prakash Jawale

A bilingual (English + Marathi) book on Vedic astrology, written around one real birth chart — 22 October 1996, 12:36 AM, Savda (Maharashtra). 33 chapters across 7 parts. Intended as a beginner's textbook that doubles as a personal natal-chart study.

## Structure

- **Part I — Foundations** (ch01–ch05): how to read, birth details, rashis, nakshatras, houses
- **Part II — Your Chart** (ch06–ch15): grahas, Cancer Lagna, Aquarius Moon, each planet by house, house-by-house read
- **Part III — Yogas & Doshas** (ch16–ch17): Harsha, Neecha Bhanga, Bhavan Prapti; mild Manglik, Sade Sati
- **Part IV — Time & Dasha** (ch18–ch24): Vimshottari, Mars MD, Rahu MD, Jupiter MD (current), Saturn MD, Mercury MD, other MDs
- **Part V — Transits & Divisions** (ch25–ch27): Sade Sati phases, Gochar, Navamsa & Dasamsa
- **Part VI — Life Domains** (ch28–ch31): marriage, career & business, health, children
- **Part VII — Closing** (ch32–ch33): remedies + path forward, glossary
